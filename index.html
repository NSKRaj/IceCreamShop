About Us

Our journey began with a simple love last for 3 years

Our journey began with a simple love for the pure joy that ice cream brings to people of all ages. Established in 2020, we set out to create a space where families, friends, and ice cream enthusiasts could come together to indulge in delightful frozen treats. We wanted to create a haven where the rich, creamy goodness of our ice cream could be savored, and memories could be made.
